Gyan Ganga International Academy Bhopal

Gyan Ganga International Academy is a Co-Ed Residential and Day Boarding School affiliated to CBSE, Delhi founded in 1996 at Hoshangabad Road, Bhopal. Gyan Ganga had perceived quality in all aspects sphering around the educational framework. The pursuit of quality has been the paramount consideration. The commitment for quality can easily be observed from the essence and gesture of educational dynamics. The Gyan Ganga clan has accepted the formidable task of educating & igniting the students' mind globally ranging from Kindergarten to Post Graduate classes.
Vision
To prepare the student for achieving overall development of personality & to become a responsible citizen with virtues like Self-discipline, sense of duty, enthusiasm, and endeavor to acquire physical, moral & spiritual attainments.
To achieve their goals opportunities are provided to the students to think for themselves and develop individuality.
Mission
We are dedicated to prepare our students as responsible citizens with a sense of integrity and honesty, spirit of adventure and exploration, research and creation, understanding and respect of the rich cultural heritage of India. We believe in fostering each individual's potentialities and therefore provide varied opportunities for successful learning and growth. To achieve this goal we provide opportunities for the students to take initiatives, to think for themselves and to develop myriad interests
