Gyan Ganga International School

A unique international educational institution situated near Bheraghat Bypass Square, Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh. With an emphasis on all round development of a child, it has carved out a curriculum that lays equal emphasis on academics and personality development, thus known as one of the best day cum residential school of India.
We believe in enabling an individual to develop into a complete human being. To ensure the objective, each student's course of study is carefully planned and followed. Those with outstanding qualities are given opportunities to shine and not rust unfurnished, and others lend a helping hand with understanding to reach their potential. Every possible effort is made, to recognize the significant factors of academic pursuit, maturing experience and enthusiasm to learn.
Our philosophy aims to lead students to realize their capabilities and build them into real skills and find the best possible educational frame. The daily life is structured in a manner that enhances the individual learning process, thus creating an ideal educational environment 'Where the mind is without fear and the head is held high'.

  • Focus and values the academics
    At GGIS academic achievement is valued and supported. Our program seeks to balance physical, emotional, social and spiritual growth with academics. We develop in our students the qualities of optimism, confidence, and enthusiasm, concern for justice, and idealism, all of which will metamorphose into growth, maturity and global perspective that citizens of the 21st century should posses. All this help us build a strong place in the list of best schools in Madhya Pradesh. GGIS Ranks no. 32 in India, no. 5 in Madhya Pradesh & no. 1 in Jabalpur.
  • Life at GGIS
    he personal commitment of Gyan Ganga International School is the foundation of our success. The School's house structure plays an important role in individual development, helping to build camaraderie and fostering a sense of belonging. As a result, they are uniquely positioned to oversee both pastoral and academic progress.
    We respect the principle of each individual being unique, and celebrate the integrity and dignity of each student.
    Discipline at GGIS is based upon:
     Respect for self
     Consideration for others
     Concern for the environment
     Sports and Hobby Clubs with vast infrastructure
  • Financial Aid & Benefits
    Financial Aid is granted to students whose parents are unable to afford the fee of the school due to financial constraints. These grants are liberally available to the meritorious or deserving cases; there is an overall budget within which the system functions. GGIS also offers cash prizes worth 4 lacs to the toppers of every class every year.
  • Celebrating Children's Day with President of India
    GGIS has been the only school in Jabalpur getting the opportunity continuously for last 3 years to visit Rashtrapati Bhawan & meet His Excellency Presidents of India for three consecutive years. 24 students and 3 teachers from GGIS were lucky enough to celebrate children's day with president till now.
  • Career Guidance & Counseling
    Career counseling seminar and workshop are being conducted every year for selection of subject or discipline after class X by expert career counselors. Students are being guided for competitive exams through special coaching programmes conducted by the members of the staff and the counselors. The Library has sufficient number of volumes of reference books & journals to help students to prepare for the various examinations. In pursuance of excellence and professional success of our students GGIS have initiated Competitive Coaching Classes for JEE AIPMT; CA and CS in school campus.
  • International Youth Exchange Programme
    Gyan Ganga International School, Jabalpur has tied up with organization “Youth For Understanding Various Countries”. GGIS is a single true International School in Jabalpur, who has started student exchange programme with Germany. A student Anna Berlin joined the institution for one academic session 2015-16 to understand the education system and culture of our country. She is studying in class 11 and students of GGIS can visit 24 countries under same programme. Under this programe where students from GGIS would be going to different countries at the same time for academic session 2016-17 two students from Germany & Switzerland would be coming to join Gyan Ganga International School, Jabalpur.
